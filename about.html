IF YOU CAN DREAM || "YOU CAN DO IT"

Samiun Mahmud, a 23-year-old Bangladeshi student, has overcome numerous struggles throughout their education. With the unwavering support of their parents, AKM Abdullah al Mahmud and Tasmina Sultana, and the inspiration of a research partner, Samiun excelled in the field of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at IUB. The CSE department provided an ideal environment, combining excellent faculty, resources, and lab facilities. Through participation in contests, festivals, workshops, and internships, Samiun gained practical experience and built confidence. Their journey as a robotics specialist continues with gratitude and a determination to make a meaningful impact.
